  • Session: Outsider
I honour the outsider in me — the one who creates without asking for permission. I do not need to be understood by the system to be true to myself. My work is valid, even when it’s unseen. I carry beauty in my madness, and meaning in my isolation. I walk my own path, not to rebel, but because it’s mine. I am the artist that doesn’t fit in — and that is my power.
